About Me.

My name is Aya Al-Shamkhee and I am currently a student of Graphic Design
at Algonquin College.

Through my studies, I have developed a solid foundation in design
principles and have gained experience with a variety of design tools,

and Sketch.

I am passionate about typography, layout design, and branding. I have
also had the opportunity to experiment with other areas of design, such as
motion graphics and web design.
